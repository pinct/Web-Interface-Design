The Oculus Rift
Travel to new worlds with the Oculus Rift. The Oculus offers a new type of gaming experience. Take control over your gaming experience in a new way, by picking up your Oculus Touch controllers, and immerse yourself in the world of VR. Get even closer to the action and experience a truly fantastic VR experience. The Oculus has been designed to be the most comfortable VR experience, with adjustable headbands and comforting padding. The Oculus supports hundreds of games across many genres including RPGs, shooters, puzzle games and more. The Oculus also had intregrated high defenition headphones to acompany the VR experience.
