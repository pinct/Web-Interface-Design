Websites About Protected Areas' Issues

Protected areas are marked pieces of nature that managed through convervation and legal protection to preserve nature and cultural values. They are incredibly important to many people's lives, influencing everything from water supply to medicine. They even have been shown to influence climate change. Properly maintained protected areas are critical to maintaining biodiversity in the world.
